Pavel Maklyarovsky /
Pavel Maklyarovsky is a visual artist, videographer and short video director. Works mainly with fashion brands, musicians, artists and creates art projects. Often refers to nostalgia and daydreaming in his works. Clip montage, blurred portraits, dissolved dreamy images, optical distortions and light effects characterise his works which can be seen in fashion media, social accounts of influencers and brands, contemporary art exhibitions.
