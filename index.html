Pavel Maklyarovsky

Pavel Maklyarovsky is a visual artist, videographer and short video director. He works mainly with fashion brands, musicians and artists, and creates art projects.

His work often refers to nostalgia and daydreaming: clip montage, blurred portraits, dissolved images, optical distortions and light effects.
